# Mini-Project: Safety Policy Evaluation

## Goal
Evaluate a safety policy against a small set of traces and document failures.

## Steps
1. Run the demo script to generate traces.
2. Identify which traces violate the safety rule.
3. Document the failing trace and propose a fix.

## Suggested Commands
- `python3 modules/05-autonomous-systems/03-implementations/python/src/demo.py`

## Success Criteria
- Clear identification of violating traces.
- Proposed policy change to eliminate violations.
