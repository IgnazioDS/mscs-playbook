# Concepts

Read these pages in numerical order. Later pages assume the workflow discipline
and representation choices established by the earlier ones.

- [01 Data Mining Pipeline Overview](01-data-mining-pipeline-overview.md): the end-to-end workflow that connects profiling, preparation, mining, evaluation, and reporting.
- [02 Data Understanding and Profiling](02-data-understanding-and-profiling.md): how to inspect schema, missingness, duplicates, and distributions before transformation.
- [03 Cleaning, Missingness, and Outliers](03-cleaning-missingness-and-outliers.md): task-aware strategies for fixing or stabilizing problematic data.
- [04 Feature Engineering and Encoding](04-feature-engineering-and-encoding.md): how representation choices shape the mining geometry and search space.
- [05 Model Selection and Validation](05-model-selection-and-validation.md): the validation logic that keeps discovered structure from being overclaimed.
- [06 Clustering and Evaluation](06-clustering-and-evaluation.md): segmentation methods, internal metrics, stability checks, and profile interpretation.
- [07 Anomaly Detection Basics](07-anomaly-detection-basics.md): how to define normal behavior, rank unusual cases, and calibrate alert thresholds.
- [08 Association Rules: Apriori and FP-Growth](08-association-rules-apriori-and-fp-growth.md): transactional co-occurrence mining and rule evaluation with support, confidence, and lift.
- [09 Experiment Tracking and Reproducibility](09-experiment-tracking-and-reproducibility.md): the metadata and artifact discipline that makes mining results auditable and comparable.
